# Stakeholder Profiles

This reference provides a deep dive into the values, concerns, and typical questions of different stakeholder groups.

## Technical Stakeholders
*The Builders and Guardians*

- **Primary Values:** Scalability, performance, security, maintainability, simplicity.
- **Key Concerns:** Technical debt, system complexity, breaking changes, resource constraints.
- **Typical Questions:**
    - "How does this scale?"
    - "What are the security implications?"
    - "How much effort is required to maintain this?"
    - "Does this align with our current architecture?"
- **Keywords:** Latency, Throughput, Refactoring, CI/CD, Infrastructure, Security Patch.

## Product Stakeholders
*The User Advocates*

- **Primary Values:** User satisfaction, ease of use, feature value, time-to-market.
- **Key Concerns:** UX friction, feature bloat, missing user needs, lack of differentiation.
- **Typical Questions:**
    - "How does this solve the user's problem?"
    - "Is the flow intuitive?"
    - "When can we ship the MVP?"
    - "How does this compare to competitors?"
- **Keywords:** User Journey, Persona, Friction, MVP, Engagement, Retention.

## Business Stakeholders
*The Strategic Drivers*

- **Primary Values:** Revenue, profit, market share, strategic alignment, risk mitigation.
- **Key Concerns:** Opportunity cost, legal liability, budget overruns, brand reputation.
- **Typical Questions:**
    - "What is the projected ROI?"
    - "Does this align with our Q3 goals?"
    - "What are the risks of NOT doing this?"
    - "How does this impact our bottom line?"
- **Keywords:** ROI, EBITDA, Market Penetration, Compliance, Strategic Pillar, CAC.
