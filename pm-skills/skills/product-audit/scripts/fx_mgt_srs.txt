1. OverviewFX Management is a service that allows the system to store and retrieve foreign exchange (FX) rates used in payment transactions involving currency conversions. It is designed to support integration with other payment providers that don't offer dynamic FX quotations.2. ObjectivesEnable storing (inserting) FX quotations.Provide reliable APIs to retrieve FX quotations based on quoteId or currency pairs.Allow conversion from base currency amount to quote currency amount using FX rates.3. How to show rateValue on UIWhy do we use baseCurrency and baseUnit?We use baseCurrency and baseUnit to provide a clear and structured representation for both users and UI tools. This approach simplifies the display of currency pairs, especially when an operator inputs a currency pair with a large number of decimal places. Without this structure, the values shown to users might be confusing or difficult to interpret. By defining a base currency and its corresponding unit, we ensure that the information is presented in a way that is logical, easy to understand, and user-friendly.ExamplesExample 1 (Normal case):Example:FieldValuebaseCurrencyUSDbaseUnit1currencyPairUSD/VNDrateValue25.892baseMinorAmount100Display on UI:Rate: 1 USD = 25.892 VNDBase Amount in Decimal: 100 / 10^2 = 1.00 USDImportant note for implementation:Please always parse baseUnitString as numeric before calculation.The formula is linear and scale-free - works regardless of what value baseUnit (always not 0).For baseUnit amount of base currency, the equivalent quote currency is rateValue.
